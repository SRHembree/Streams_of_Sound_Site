Where Is My Money Going?

After you click “Donate”, funds are distributed in three ways. The first is to our artists, who schedule with us to perform at the various hospitals and care facilities around Jacksonville, FL.

Second, donations are funneled into new instruments that are used to expand our therapeutic purposes to other hospitals and care facilities in the are.

The last third of the funds are used to pay for operating costs, such as website maintenance, background checks for our artists, and training.
